Welcome to 1991!

Pearl Jam, Soundgarden, Mariah Carey, Metallica, U2, the Red Hot Chili Peppers, Nirvana, and Prince... These are all musical powerhouses who released
an album in the year 1991. Many artists can claim their rise to fame to the album they released in this one year. Something must have truly been in the water
this year... Some of the artists who got their start in this year include the grunge bands Pearl Jam and Nirvana. However, the talent, long-lasting relevance, and
success does not stop there. Read through the pages on this website to learn more about the year that defined grunge music and created some of the most widely-known
artists that continue to stand the test of time.
